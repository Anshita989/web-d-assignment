Rasmalai

Soft cottage cheese patties soaked in thickened, flavored milk and garnished with nuts.

Prep time: 45 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full‑fat milk (for chenna)
  • 1 liter milk (for rabri)
  • 3–4 tbsp sugar (for chenna balls)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for rabri)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds
  • 1–2 tsp lemon juice or vinegar

Step‑by‑Step Instructions

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle, then strain and wash to get soft chenna.
  2. Knead chenna until smooth and shape into small flat discs.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with water and sugar, then gently cook chenna discs until they puff up.
  4. For rabri, simmer milk until it reduces, add sugar, saffron, and cardamom.
  5. Squeeze extra syrup from cooked discs and place them in warm rabri.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for a few hours and garnish with nuts before serving.
