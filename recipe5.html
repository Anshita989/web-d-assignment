Seekh Kabab

Juicy minced meat skewers flavored with spices and grilled to perfection.

Prep time: 45 mins Difficulty: Hard

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced meat (mutton or chicken)
  • 1 onion, very finely chopped
  • Green chilies and coriander leaves, chopped
  • Ginger‑garlic paste
  • Garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder
  • Salt and lemon juice
  • Skewers (metal or wooden)

Step‑by‑Step Instructions

  1. Mix minced meat with onions, spices, ginger‑garlic paste, salt, and herbs.
  2. Knead the mixture well until it becomes sticky and holds shape.
  3. Take a portion and press it evenly around a skewer, forming a long kabab.
  4. Cook on a grill, tawa, or in an oven, brushing with oil or ghee.
  5. Turn occasionally until kababs are cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion rings.
