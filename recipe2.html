Pav Bhaji

Street‑style spicy mashed vegetable curry served with buttered pav.

Prep time: 40 mins Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, cauliflower)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum, chopped
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Butter, salt, and lemon wedges
  • Pav buns

Step‑by‑Step Instructions

  1. Boil vegetables until soft and mash them lightly.
  2. In a pan, heat butter and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and capsicum, cook until soft.
  4. Mix in pav bhaji masala, chili powder, and salt.
  5. Add boiled vegetables and some water, mash and simmer.
  6. Toast pav with butter on a tawa and serve hot with bhaji, onions, and lemon.
