Chicken Lababdar

Rich and creamy chicken curry with a tomato‑cashew base and aromatic spices.

Prep time: 60 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken (boneless or with bone)
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 10–12 cashews, soaked
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • Ginger‑garlic paste
  • Red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder
  • Oil, butter, and salt

Step‑by‑Step Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with salt, chili powder, and ginger‑garlic paste for 20 minutes.
  2. Sauté onions until golden, then blend with soaked cashews into a smooth paste.
  3. In a pan, cook tomato puree with spices until oil separates.
  4. Add onion‑cashew paste and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook until pieces are tender.
  6. Finish with cream and a knob of butter, simmer briefly, and serve hot.
