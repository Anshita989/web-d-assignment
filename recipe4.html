Chicken Korma

Mildly spiced chicken cooked in a creamy yogurt, nut, and spice gravy.

Prep time: 55 mins Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 onions, fried and crushed
  • 10–12 cashews or almonds, soaked and ground
  • Ginger‑garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric
  • Oil/ghee and salt

Step‑by‑Step Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger‑garlic paste, salt, and spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee, add whole spices, then add marinated chicken.
  3. Cook until chicken changes color and yogurt starts to release oil.
  4. Add nut paste and crushed fried onions, mix well.
  5. Cover and cook on low flame until chicken is tender and gravy thick.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve with naan or rice.
